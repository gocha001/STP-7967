FAQ

Epic Fantasy is a turn-based RPG set in a magical world full of quests, challenges, and mysterious characters. You’ll engage in strategic battles, uncover powerful artifacts, and face epic foes.

Yes, Epic Fantasy is free to download and play. However, it features in-app purchases to enhance gameplay and access exclusive content.

You can level up by completing quests, defeating enemies, and collecting resources. Use these to improve your hero's abilities, unlock new skills, and upgrade your gear.

Yes, you can enjoy many aspects of the game offline, but certain features like special events or daily rewards may require an internet connection.

New areas and dungeons unlock as you progress through the story. Completing major quests and defeating tough enemies is key to advancing.

In-app purchases allow players to buy special items, powerful upgrades, and cosmetics to enhance gameplay. These are not required to enjoy the game, but they provide a quicker route to progress.

Our Uniqueness

What sets Epic Fantasy apart from other RPGs is its seamless blend of epic storytelling, tactical combat, and an immersive world that feels alive.

Unlike many fantasy games, Epic Fantasy offers a deep narrative that evolves with your choices, ensuring every journey is unique. The game isn’t just about fighting—it’s about discovering hidden lore, forging alliances, and unearthing powerful secrets.

Whether you’re battling monsters or solving ancient puzzles, every part of this game is designed to make you feel like the hero of your own legendary story.
